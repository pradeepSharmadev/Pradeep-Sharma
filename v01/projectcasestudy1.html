UX Research & Design

College Networking
Days

Role

Product Designer

Timeline

4 Months

Client

Internal University Project

Bridging the gap between academic learning and industry networking.

Growing up in India, my fascination with art and its potential to influence lives has been a lifelong passion. This love for artistic expression led me to explore digital mediums where technology meets design.

The primary challenge was creating a seamless networking experience for students who often feel overwhelmed by professional platforms. We needed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry networking without the intimidation factor.

Process

01.

Discovery & Research

In-depth user interviews and market analysis to identify pain points in current student networking habits.

02.

Ideation & Flow

Mapping out the ecosystem and defining the core mechanics of low-pressure interaction.

03.

Prototyping

Iterative design cycles focusing on a gamified mobile-first experience.

04.

Validation

Usability testing with 20+ students to refine the matching algorithm UI.

Quantifiable Impact

40%

Increase in engagement

200+

Mentorships formed

The project resulted in a significant shift in student confidence. Users reported feeling more prepared to initiate professional conversations, and the mentoring system proved vital for junior student growth.
