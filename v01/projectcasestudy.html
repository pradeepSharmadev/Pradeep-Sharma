Overview

Growing up in India, my fascination with art and its potential to influence lives has been a lifelong passion. I began sketching and painting at the age of eight, and this love for artistic expression led me to explore digital mediums where technology meets design.

With a focus on experience design and business strategy, I have developed strong skills in design research, concept development, prototyping, and user testing. My objective has always been to unify the team with a shared vision and deliver impactful user-centric solutions.

Challenges and solution

The primary challenge was creating a seamless networking experience for students who often feel overwhelmed by professional platforms. We needed to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry networking without the intimidation factor.

Our solution involved a gamified networking interface that encouraged interaction through shared interests and low-pressure conversation starters. We implemented a robust mentoring system that paired seniors with juniors based on skill sets and career goals.
