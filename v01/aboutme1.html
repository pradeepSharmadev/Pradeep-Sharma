About.

Based in Bangalore, I am a product designer dedicated to the pursuit of essential beauty and functional clarity in digital spaces.

Philosophy

I believe that design is not just a solution to a problem, but a silent dialogue between the creator and the user. My approach is rooted in the "less is more" ideology—not as a stylistic choice, but as a commitment to removing friction and noise.

With nearly a decade of experience, I’ve learned that the most complex challenges often require the simplest executions. Whether I'm architecting a design system or refining a micro-interaction, my goal remains the same: to create products that feel as though they've always existed.
