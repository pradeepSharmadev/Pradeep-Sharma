Who am I?

Growing up in India, my fascination with art and its potential to influence lives has been a lifelong passion. I began sketching and painting at the age of eight, and this love for artistic expression led me to the world of design.

Throughout my career, I have worked on a variety of products and teams. These experiences encompassed serving as the sole designer for a start-up, crafting user-friendly fin-tech interactions at Quick, revamping intricate and engaging features at Rocket, and collaborating within cross-functional teams at Google.

With a focus on experience design and business strategy, I have developed strong skills in design research, concept development, prototyping, and user testing. My objective has always been to unify the team with a shared vision.
